Abstract Art is

Art that doesn't represent reality, but seeks to copy it using shapes, forms, colors, and textures. Abstract art uses visual language of shape, form, color and line to create a piece which exists with a independence from the world.

It's not rocket science, it's computer science

With Brushe, you can easily convert photos to images that are a different form of art, which lets you get more out of your photos. All you need to do is get your image in a readable format and then upload it onto our page, where it will be rendered in real time while you watch. Not only is the end result a nice painting, the real time rendering creates a spectacle for you to watch while you wait for the final product. Whether you post them to your social media, or just want nice things to look at, you can always get more with Brushe.
