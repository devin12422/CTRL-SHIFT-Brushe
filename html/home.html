Brushe

Generative impressionism written in node.js. This website allows you to have a bit of fun with art and no effort needed.

The script detects contrast, and the lines gravitate to areas with higher contrast.

This means the more different the border between two colors are, the more it will get drawn

So a picture with more different colors will get drawn in more detail.
