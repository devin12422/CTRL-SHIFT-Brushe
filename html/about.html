About Brushe

  • I actually came up with the idea for this website last year during a mentoring
  • session. There was a volunteer who introduced himself and said that he
  • made procedural art for fun. The idea interested me, and I googled it later.
  • After the competition was over, I did some research and decided I would make it
  • my project for this year. I did some research, found some code, a few guides,
  • and a few websites. Then, I put this website together for the competition.
  • I decided I wanted to do something interesting for the website, and after
  • asking for a bit of advice, I decided to make a website entirely about
  • Skeuomorphism. Thats when elements of your website look like objects in real
  • life that have the same function. So I made my website look like a bedroom.
  • A bedroom thats a lot cleaner than my bedroom.
